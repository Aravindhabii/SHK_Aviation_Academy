ACADEMICS

BBA Airlines and Airport Management

India’s Aviation Industry is expected to witness Rs 35,000 crore (US$ 4.99 billion) investment in the next four years. SHK AVIATION ACADEMY’s BBA in Airline and Airport Management degree will help students get ahead in the high-powered aviation industry. This course caters to those students who are seeking careers in management and operation in the airside activities of the aviation industry. This course explores all facets of business administration and management. It is a 3 years long Undergraduate program. BBA program in Airport Management trains students to take on managerial as well as administrative roles in an Airport or in the Aviation sector. The BBA in Airport Management program has been designed to train students manage staff, deal with technical sections like Air Traffic and Control, maintenance staff, cargo department and coordinate them all, manage finances etc.

Eligibility Criteria

Students who have passed 12th standard board examinations (any stream Science/Arts/Commerce) are eligible to apply for this course .

For the Degree:

The candidates shall have subsequently undergone the prescribed programme of study in a institute for not less than three academic years, passed the examinations prescribed and fulfil such conditions as have been prescribed therefore.

Admission:

Admission is based on the marks in the qualifying examination.

Duration of the course:

The course shall extend over a period of Three years under SEMESTER pattern accounting to six SEMESTERs.

Career

BBA Aviation graduates have different career paths and opportunities available in front of them. Prominent job roles available within the aviation industry are –

  • Airport Manager/Assistant Manager
  • Cargo Manager
  • Ground Staff Manager
  • Safety and Maintenance Manager
  • Customer Care Executive

BSC Aviation

SHK AVIATION ACADEMY is the nexus of aviation education, students benefit from much more than prestige; they learn from experienced faculty who share first-hand knowledge in aviation and who are at the forefront in the developing field of aircraft. B.Sc. Aviation course is related to the field of Aviation Science. Aviation science deals with the study, design and operation of flight capable machine like aircraft, helicopters, gliders , etc. The Bachelor of Science in Aviation program provides graduates with the necessary skills and knowledge needed to become industry leaders in a variety of aviation related fields, including pilots, sensor operators, mission planners and ground technicians. Students study the spectrum of the industry including hazardous operations, government regulations, surveillance, data collection, and even the engineering aspects. Since B.Sc. Aviation course covers diverse topics like design, production and operation of aircraft, it offers diverse job opportunities to graduates.

Eligibility Criteria

Students who have passed 10+2 Science stream from a recognized board with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics subjects are eligible to pursue this course.

For the Degree:

The candidates shall have subsequently undergone the prescribed programme of study in a institute for not less than three academic years, passed the examinations prescribed and fulfill such conditions as have been prescribed therefore.

Admission:

Admission is based on the marks in the qualifying examination.

Duration of the course:

The course shall extend over a period of Three years under NON SEMESTER pattern accounting to three SEMESTERs.

Career

B.Sc. Aviation graduates have different career paths and opportunities available in front of them. Prominent job roles available within the aviation industry are –

  • Pilot (must possess CPL)
  • Flight attendant
  • Air Traffic Controller
  • Aircraft maintenance professional
  • Cargo manager
  • Airport ground duty manager

MBA Airlines and Airport Management

The Master of Business Administration in Aviation degree provides strong foundations for success in any facet of the global aviation business arena. It provides traditional business skills, but is unique in its focus on the aviation. Though the degree provides specialized insight into the global business environment, it sets up graduates for success in non-aviation industries as well. The courses within the program deliver the required MBA core content while investigating trends in the key industry segments in the world of aviation.The curriculum has been developed, and continues to evolve with close attention paid to the demands of the industry. Most of the courses contain a research component that will provide experience and an insider’s examination of global business practices. The program culminates with a capstone course for which students will write a strategic business plan. Students get the opportunity to acquire the abilities that would assist them to come up with the regularly evolving industry. Graduates in Aviation management are adept at discerning the critical elements of flight, ground, cost adequacy in addition to administration aptitudes to foresee and govern the business difficulties and open the doors for the blooming of the organization.

Eligibility Criteria

Candidate for admission to MBA in Airline and Airport Management shall be required to have passed any bachelor degree from recognized University/Institution.

For the Degree:

The candidates shall have subsequently undergone the prescribed programme of study in a institute for not less than two academic years comprising 4 semesters, passed the examinations prescribed and fulfill such conditions as have been prescribed therefore.

Admission:

Admission based on the marks in the qualifying examination.

Duration of the course:

The course shall extend over a period of two years under semester pattern accounting to four semesters.

Career

The various job roles that Students can have upon successful completion of MBA Airlines and Airport Management are mentioned below

  • Aviation Planner
  • Customer Relationship Manager – Airlines
  • Financial Analyst for airlines
  • Operations Analyst for airlines
  • Purchasing Manager for airlines
  • Senior Staff Analyst for airlines
  • Trainer
  • Territory Manager – Corporate Sales
  • Airport Manager
  • HR Manager
  • Finance Manager
  • Cargo Manager
  • Customer Service Executive
  • Asst Manager

BSC AIRCRAFT MAINTENANCE

This course prepares students in the Technical aspects of Aviation, especially in Maintenance, Repair and Overhauling, Airlines, Airports and other Aviation Technical matters. The students are exposed to Live Operational Aircraft during the course at facilities of Indigo Airlines and Airworks India Engineering. The course helps students learn the fundamentals of Aeronautical Science specifically in reference to Aircraft Operations. It also aims to develop Student’s interest in Aeronautics and related fields. It develop the skills to identify problems that may be detrimental to the safe operations of an Aircraft in the quickest and safest possible manner. To understand that Safety and Quality cannot be compromised. The course aims at holistic development, intellectual nourishment, quality education, emotional stability, ability to face the challenges in a competitive. Other curricular, co-curricular,extra-curricular and sports activities are well-planned in a calendar of events to bring out the best in a Student.

Objective:

The 3 years Bachelor Degree BSc (Aircraft Maintenance) is offered with the following Students are encouraged to take part in classroom presentations, seminars and workshops. Assignments, periodical tests and exams ensure effective learning experiences for the students. Regular student feedbacks on teaching are followed up with remedial teaching and guest lecturers to keep pace with recent developments in the aviation industry.

Eligibility Criteria

A pass in the Higher Secondary Examination (HSC) conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu, or an examination accepted as equivalent thereto by the Syndicate. Candidate for admission to B.Sc Aircraft Maintenance Science (AMS) shall be required to have passed qualifying examination with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM).

For the Degree:

The candidates shall have subsequently undergone the prescribed programme of study in a institute for not less than three academic years, passed the examinations prescribed and fulfill such conditions as have been prescribed therefore.

Admission:

Admission is based on the marks in the qualifying examination.

Duration of the course:

The course shall extend over a period of Three years under semester pattern accounting to six semesters.

Career

The various job roles that Students can have upon successful completion of BSc Aircraft Maintenance are mentioned below

  • Line Maintenance
  • Base Maintenance
  • Logistics and Supply Chain Management
  • Aircraft Operation Management
  • Planning of Aircraft Servicing, Crew, Production, Flight Operations, Technical Services.
  • Ground Equipment Engineering & Management
  • Corporate Fleet Management
  • Air Traffic Control
  • Aircraft Dispatcher
  • Flight Engineer or Pilot
  • In-flight crew
  • Load-Master
  • Scheduling & Network Planning
  • Technical Publications and Documentations
